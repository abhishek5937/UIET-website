PLACEMENT POLICY

  1. A student can get maximum of 2 jobs/ 2 job letters.
  2. A student can take the first two jobs in either of the following ways:
    1. 1 Core Job and 2nd Non-Core job
    2. 1 Core and 2nd KPO job
    3. 1 Non-Core and 2nd core job
    4. 1 Non-Core and 2nd KPO job
    5. 1 Non-Core and 2nd with a positive package difference of 1.5 lakhs or more.
  3. It is mandatory for every student to have 75% attendance in all events and tests conducted by TPC. If a student fails to have 75%attendence he/she can be debarred from placements. If 3/4th majority of the TPC agree on any decision, then it will be considered final.
  4. If a student does not appear to complete the selection process of a company after applying for a company then he/she will be debarred for next two companies in which he/she is eligible.
  5. It is mandatory for every student to have 75% attendance in Infosys campus connect soft skills classes. Every student should be certified by Infosys in soft skills otherwise the student will be debarred from all placements.
  6. A student can get only 1 Training Offer Through Campus Placements.
